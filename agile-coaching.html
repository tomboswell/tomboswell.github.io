Agile coaching and facilitation

I am a ICAgile Agile Coach (ICP-ACC) & Team Facilitator (ICP-ATF).

Team Facilitator

I facilitate Agile practices and daily interactions to foster collaboration and healthy self-organisation

  • I design and facilitate purposeful meetings with clear and defined outcomes
  • I create collaborative and psychologically safe environments to encourage full participation and equality in distribution of conversational turn-taking
  • I adjust my facilitation style to meet the maturity and needs of the group (including remote teams)

Agile Coach

I work from an Agile coaching stance blending: facilitating, teaching, mentoring and professional coaching

  • Teaching and training groups in Agile processes and practices
  • Facilitating workshops and meetings
  • Coaching teams towards high performance. Encouraging team collaboration, trust and conflict resolution
  • Non-directive coaching. Helping team members set their own goals and commit to actions
