Links

Below is a curated list of free tools, books, videos, whitepapers, etc, which I have found useful as an Agile Coach.

Books (free)

  • The Agile Fluency Model, by Diane Larsen and James Shore - A short eBook outling the Agile Fluency Model
  • Kanban and Scrum - Making the Most of Both, by Henrik Kniberg and Mattias Skarin
  • Scrum and XP from the Trenches, 2nd edition, by Henrik Kniberg

    Guides

  • Kanban Guide for Scrum Teams - Scrum.orgs' guide to using Kanban with Scrum
  • Kanban University Guide - Kanban University's Official Guide to Kanban
  • Scrum Guide current version - The 2020 Scrum Guide
  • Scrum Guide past versions - An archive of previous Scrum Guides

    Tools

  • Fun Retrospectives - This website contains a range of Retro Activities that you can facilitate for your teams
  • Scrum Checklist by Henrik Kniberg - A simple tool to help access your current implentation of Scrum
  • Story Splitting Guide by Richard Lawrence and Peter Green - A great reference for Splitting User Stories
  • Team Canvas - A canvas for running Team Charter workshops
