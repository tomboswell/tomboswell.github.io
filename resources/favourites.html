My Favourite Agile Resources

Below is a curated list of free tools, books, videos, whitepapers, etc, which I have found interesting or useful as an Agile Coach. This list will be continually updated.

Articles

  • The Agile Coaches' Coach Shares Her View on SAFe - Lyssa Adkins' 2014 article on SAFe
  • Failed #SquadGoals - An insiders critique of the Spotify model
  • A Leaders Framework for Decision Making - 2007 Harvard Business Review article by David Snowden and Mary Boone, oulining the Cynefin Model
    •

    Books (Free)

  • The Agile Fluency Model, by Diane Larsen and James Shore - A short eBook outling the Agile Fluency Model
  • Kanban and Scrum - Making the Most of Both, by Henrik Kniberg and Mattias Skarin
  • Scrum and XP from the Trenches, 2nd edition, by Henrik Kniberg
    •

    Cheatsheets and Graphics

  • Scrum Framework at a Glance - One page graphic outlining Scrum by Scrum Alliance
    •

    Courses and Learning Paths (Free)

  • Scrum Master Learning Path - Free online Learning Path for Scrum Masters by Scrum.org
    •

    Guides

  • Kanban Guide for Scrum Teams - Scrum.orgs' guide to using Kanban with Scrum
  • Kanban University Guide - Kanban University's Official Guide to Kanban
  • Scrum Guide current version - The 2020 Scrum Guide
  • Scrum Guide past versions - An archive of previous Scrum Guides
    •

    Tools, models, canvases, etc

  • Scrum Checklist by Henrik Kniberg - A simple tool to help access your current implentation of Scrum
  • Story Splitting Guide by Richard Lawrence and Peter Green - A great reference for Splitting User Stories
  • Team Canvas - A canvas for running Team Charter workshops
    •

    Videos

  • Agile Product Ownership in a Nutshell (15 mins YouTube video) by Henrik Kniberg
  • MindSpring Presents: "Greatness" by David Marquet (9 mins YouTube video)
  • SAFe Explained in Five Minutes (5 mins YouTube video) - A very quick overview of SAFe 5.0 (Note: The current version of SAFe is 6.0, you can read about those updates here
  • Scrum: How to do twice as much in half the time, Jeff Sutherland, TEDxAix (15 mins YouTube video)

    Websites

  • Fun Retrospectives - This website contains a range of Retro Activities that you can facilitate for your teams
  • Liberating Structures - This website outlines 33 microstructures for facilitating meetings and conversations
  • Scrum PLOP - The Home of the Scrum Pattern Community

  • Scaling Agile @ Spotify, by Henrik Kniberg and Anders Ivarsson - The original 2012 paper
